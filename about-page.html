About Me

Hello! I'm Samantha Shomo, a game programmer and data scientist who graduated from Champlain College in 2025. I specialize in gameplay, networking, and AI programming, machine learning, and data privacy.

My journey in game development began with a fascination for creating interactive experiences that bring joy and excitement to players. I've since developed expertise in various game engines and programming languages, always with an eye toward crafting engaging player experiences.

When I'm not coding, you might find me playing the latest indie games, hanging out with my cats, or exploring the outdoors for inspiration.

I believe that games have the power to connect people, tell powerful stories, and even change perspectives. My goal is to contribute to this medium by creating memorable, meaningful experiences.
