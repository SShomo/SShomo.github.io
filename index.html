About Me

Hello! I'm Samantha Shomo, a feline-inspired game programmer with over 5 years of experience in the game development industry. I specialize in gameplay programming, engine development, and procedural generation.

My journey began with a Computer Science degree from Whisker University, where I developed a deep understanding of algorithms, data structures, and software architecture. Since then, I've worked with indie teams and AAA studios alike, contributing to titles that have reached millions of players worldwide.

I'm driven by the curiosity of solving complex technical problems while creating engaging player experiences. My goal is to pounce on the boundaries of what's possible in interactive entertainment through innovative code and elegant solutions.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me playing the latest indie games, contributing to open-source projects, or napping in sunny spots with my cat companions, Pixel and Byte.
